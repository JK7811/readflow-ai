The Murder of Jacqueline Sheehan

点击每句旁边的 ▶ 可听英文；播放时该句会自动高亮。也可以调整语速或连续播放整段。

Jacqueline Sheehan disappeared in the early hours of April 15, 1998, after a night that began like hundreds before it.

1998年4月15日凌晨，Jacqueline Sheehan 在一个看似与过去无数夜晚没有不同的晚上失踪。

She had gone dancing with her boyfriend, Rodney Bice, and her mother at Suzie’s in Deltona, Florida.

她当晚与男友 Rodney Bice 及母亲一起，到佛罗里达州 Deltona 的 Suzie’s 跳舞。

Friends later said she was in good spirits that night.

朋友后来表示，她那晚心情很好。

She laughed, drank, danced, and for a few hours seemed free from the turbulence that had defined much of her relationship with Bice.

她笑着、喝酒、跳舞，在短短几个小时里，仿佛暂时摆脱了长期笼罩她与 Bice 关系的动荡。

But when the couple returned to the Willow Park Apartments in Altamonte Springs, the atmosphere changed.

但当两人回到 Altamonte Springs 的 Willow Park Apartments 后，气氛发生了变化。

According to investigators, the two argued over a car.

调查人员称，两人因为一辆车发生争吵。

Sometime around 2:30 a.m., Jacqueline stormed out of the apartment.

大约凌晨2时30分，Jacqueline 怒气冲冲地离开公寓。

Rodney Bice later claimed she walked to a nearby pay phone after leaving in anger.

Rodney Bice 后来声称，她生气离开后走向附近的公共电话。

That was the last time anyone officially reported seeing her alive.

那是最后一次有人正式报告看到她还活着。
